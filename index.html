About us

Established over 35 years ago in 1988, we began our journey to become who we are today, SEE Group.

SEE Group now comprises four businesses: SEE Civil, Civil Rent, Quarry Solutions, and DMC Quarries.

  • SEE Civil: civil construction and engineering.
  • DMC Quarries: specialty stone products for decorative and construction use.
  • Quarry Solutions: quarrying and material processing.
  • Civil Rent: complete fleet and asset solutions.

Each business exists to integrate and play a key role in delivering projects in the infrastructure, mining and resources, aviation and urban development sectors.
